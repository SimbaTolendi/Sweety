Korea Institute of Advanced Technology is South Korea's leading teaching and research university located in Daejeon. In 2011, KAIST took 90 positions in the ranking of the world's leading universities. IITU and KAIST signed a memorandum of cooperation in February 2014 and the semester student exchange is the first step in long-term and diversified cooperation between our universities.

Universiti Tenaga Nasional (UNITEN) Видение Founded in 1976, it is one of the first private universities in Malaysia and is run by Tenaga Nasional Berhad (TNB). The university has two campuses - Putrajaya Campus and Bandar Muadzam Shah. These campuses host not only academic programs, but also trainings in the field of engineering technology. The university programs are focused on areas such as engineering, information technology, business management, etc. The Putrajaya Campus consists of the College of Engineering, the College of Information Technology, and the Institute for the Humanities.

University of Applied Sciences Hof Founded in 1994, is a public non-profit business, media and technical vocational university founded in 1994 in Upper Franconia, Bavaria, Germany. Practice-orientation, internationalization and the intelligent use of resources are at the heart of teaching and research at Hof University, which currently offers 28 bachelor and master degree programs in Business, Computing and Engineering.

University of Pisa Università di Pisa in the sunny city of Pisa, in the Tuscany region is one of the oldest universities in Europe, founded in 1343. The university is in the top 5 of the best universities in Italy; known for strong training primarily in Mathematics and Physics.
